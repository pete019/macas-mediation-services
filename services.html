  • Harassment & Discrimination

Our Mediation Process

  1. Initial Consultation: We understand your situation and discuss options
  2. Agreement: All parties agree to the mediation process
  3. Joint Sessions: Parties meet to discuss the dispute
  4. Private Sessions: We work with each party individually if needed
  5. Resolution: We facilitate agreement on mutually acceptable terms
  6. Documentation: A formal agreement is drafted and signed
